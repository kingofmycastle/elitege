ABOUT THE PRESIDENT & CEO

Before launching her own company, Yesmin Asmar enjoyed a successful career in corporate America and excelled in positions at boutique firms and a national presidential campaign. Her responsibilities in the event-management field have included organizational leadership, budget preparation, on-site coordination and management of cross-functional teams. In addition, Ms. Asmar has extensive knowledge of creating integrated planning timelines for coordinating pre-event logistics, exhibit design and set-up, transportation, security and allocation of services.
