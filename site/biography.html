Headquartered in New York City, and with a satellite office in Washington D.C., the Elite Group has provided a full range of event services to a discerning clientele for more than seven years. Our mission is to create individually designed events for each of our clients’ unique needs within a controlled budget, while creating the most memorable events for them and their guests. Whether you need help managing an entire event or certain aspects of a program, our seasoned team designs and flawlessly executes the event planning process, including on-site direction the day of the event.
